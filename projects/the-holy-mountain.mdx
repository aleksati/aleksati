A captivating avant-garde pop trio from Oslo. THM blends accordion, synthesizer, drums, and vocals to create hypnotic, atmospheric compositions that exude a dark, almost mystical quality. Their music is enhanced by a unique and sublime visual aesthetic, making for unforgettable live performances.
